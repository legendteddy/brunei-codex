BruneiVerse is a practical guide site focused on everyday life in Brunei for newcomers, expats, and local readers who want direct answers.

## Editorial Principles

- Neutral tone and practical guidance
- Clear attribution and source linking
- No hidden promotions
- No political commentary or sensitive debates

## What We Cover

- Cost of living and budgeting
- Housing and setup tasks
- Banking and administrative processes
- Healthcare and daily services
- Transport and mobility

## Updates

Each guide includes a publication date and `last updated` date so readers can verify freshness.
