## OTC Safety Checklist

Check active ingredients, dosage frequency, interaction warnings, and age suitability before purchase. Keep a simple medicine log to avoid overlap across household members using similar symptom products.

### Escalation triggers

If symptoms persist, worsen, or involve high-risk signs, move from self-care to professional consultation. Early escalation is a safety control, not overreaction, when symptom pattern is unclear.
